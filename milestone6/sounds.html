Serum 2 Presets

This page highlights a collection of ambient presets designed specifically for Serum 2. These sounds are created for calm, nature-inspired music and focus on soft textures, gentle movement, and atmospheric tone.

Ethereal Garden - Ambient Serum Presets

Ethereal Garden is a collection of evolving pads, warm textures, and peaceful tones for Serum 2. Each preset is crafted to fit into quiet, emotional, or nature-themed ambient music. Soft filters, slow attacks, and spacious effects help the sounds blend smoothly into calm sound environments.
