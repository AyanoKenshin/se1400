Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

AstroScape will contain images and information about sound design, specifically space ambient sound design.

Where will you get all of the content for your site?

Almost all of it will be screenshots or videos that I will take myself of my own sound design and the information provided will be from what I have learned in over four years of practicing sound design.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

If somebody is interested in sound design, they should care about my site because the content is informative and I will showcase it in a fun and simple way.

Why should a site be built for this?

Two reasons. Sound design is hard and takes a lot of practice and learning to get to understand it. A site like this can also be used to sell sounds that I have designed and sell them in a folder all together after providing information about sound design.

What would you like users to do at your site?

Learn and get excited about sound design.

What is the goal of the site?

To show people what sound design is and even teach them about it.
